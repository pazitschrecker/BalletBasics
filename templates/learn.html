Are you ready to learn?

In ballet, there are five fundamental foot positions, first, second, third, fourth and fifth position. All steps rely on familiarity and confidence with these positions. Each foot position has a corresponding arm position.

A few of these differ slightly by ballet style (French, Bournonville, Cecchetti, Russian, etc). However, most positions remain relatively the same. Learning arm positions is important for adding grace and emotion to dance. You will first learn the foot positions and then learn the corresponding arm positions.
